Emberdelve — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-24 · com.tsorostudios.emberdelve

Emberdelve (“the app”) is a single-player, fully offline dice roguelite by Tsoro Studios (Tapiwa Makandigona).

The short version: Emberdelve collects no data. None.

Details

  • No network access. The app declares zero Android permissions — including the internet permission — so it is technically incapable of transmitting anything off your device.
  • No personal data collected. No accounts, no names, no email addresses, no contacts, no location, no identifiers.
  • No analytics, no ads, no trackers. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking code of any kind.
  • Local save data only. Your game progress (run state, unlocks, settings) is stored only in the app’s private storage on your device. Deleting the app deletes it. It never leaves your device.
  • Children. Since the app collects nothing and communicates with nothing, no data is collected from anyone, including children.

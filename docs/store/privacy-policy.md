# Emberdelve — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-24_

Emberdelve ("the app", `com.tsorostudios.emberdelve`) is a single-player,
fully offline dice roguelite by Tsoro Studios (Tapiwa Makandigona).

## The short version

**Emberdelve collects no data. None.**

## Details

- **No network access.** The app declares zero Android permissions — including
  the internet permission — so it is technically incapable of transmitting
  anything off your device.
- **No personal data collected.** No accounts, no names, no email addresses,
  no contacts, no location, no identifiers.
- **No analytics, no ads, no trackers.** The app contains no analytics SDKs,
  no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking code of any kind.
- **Local save data only.** Your game progress (run state, unlocks, settings)
  is stored only in the app's private storage on your device. Deleting the
  app deletes it. It never leaves your device.
- **Children.** Since the app collects nothing and communicates with nothing,
  no data is collected from anyone, including children.

## Google Play data-safety summary

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Does the app collect or share user data? | No |
| Data encrypted in transit? | N/A — no data leaves the device |
| Can users request data deletion? | N/A — nothing is collected; uninstalling removes local saves |

## Changes & contact

If a future version ever changes any of the above (for example, optional
cloud save), this policy will be updated first and the change called out in
the release notes.

Questions: contact the developer via the Google Play listing for Emberdelve.
